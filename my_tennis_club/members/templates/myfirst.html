Mechanical Keyboards

Discover the world of tactile typing!

What is a Mechanical Keyboard?

A mechanical keyboard uses individual mechanical switches for each key, providing a unique tactile feel and durability compared to membrane keyboards.

Types of Switches

Linear Switches

Smooth and consistent with no tactile bump, ideal for gamers who prefer quick keypresses.

Tactile Switches

Provide a tactile bump mid-press, popular among typists who appreciate feedback with each keystroke.

Clicky Switches

Produce an audible click sound along with a tactile bump, perfect for those who enjoy both sound and feedback.

Why Choose Mechanical Keyboards?

  • Durability: Lasts longer than typical membrane keyboards.
  • Customization: Offers various switch types, keycaps, and lighting options.
  • Typing Experience: Provides satisfying tactile and auditory feedback.
